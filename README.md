## ğŸ’¡ 2ï¸âƒ£ Our Solution

**Edusphere** is an intelligent educational advisor built with Generative AI logic and decision heuristics to help students choose the right academic track based on:

- Their **current education level** (Matric/O-Levels or Intermediate/A-Levels)
- **Subjects studied**
- **Marks obtained**
- **Career interests**

Students receive tailored recommendations for intermediate or university programs (like Pre-Medical, ICS, Engineering, Business, etc.) along with future career pathways. The interactive app is intuitive, educational, and localized to match Pakistanâ€™s curriculum system.
